How to make Cookie

This is webpage that explain how to make Cookie

Step 1
Prepare Ingredients

Essential Ingredients: Flour, sugar, butter, eggs, baking powder, salt, etc.

Optional Ingredients: Chocolate chips, nuts, dried fruits, or any additional ingredients you prefer.

Step 2
Make the Dough

Mix Butter and Sugar: Place room-temperature butter in a bowl and mix it with sugar until it becomes creamy and smooth.

Add Eggs: Add the eggs and mix well.

Combine Dry Ingredients: Sift together flour, baking powder, and salt, then add to the mixture, stirring until the dough is evenly combined.

Add Optional Ingredients: If desired, mix in chocolate chips or nuts.

Step 3
Shape the Cookies

Roll the dough into small balls and place them on a baking sheet with enough space between them. It’s important to leave space since the cookies will spread while baking.

Step 4
Bake

Preheat the Oven: Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).

Bake: Place the cookies in the preheated oven and bake for about 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden.

Step 5
Cool

Once the cookies are baked, remove them from the oven and let them cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a rack or plate to cool completely. This will help the cookies become crisp.
