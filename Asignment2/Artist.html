Ahn Ji-young is a Korean K-pop singer and songwriter


She is best known as the main vocalist of the group BOL4. The group has since disbanded, but she continues to perform as a solo artist while retaining the group's name. With her unique voice and refreshing charm, she has produced many hit songs.

Ahn Ji-young also possesses exceptional songwriting skills and often covers songs by other artists, showcasing her distinctive voice.

She has participated in the popular TV show "Begin Again," which primarily focuses on busking activities. The song "Cruel Summer," featured on the website, is a cover of Taylor Swift's well-known track, which she has interpreted beautifully..
